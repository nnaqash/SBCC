Meet the Volunteers!

The volunteers of the Scottish Burned Children's Club come from all different backgrounds and give the club a great personality. We asked the volunteers to write a short description about themselves on how they became involved with the club and why they stick around. Click on the images to read more about the team!

I joined the Scottish Burned Children's Club as a child when I was in an accident and ever since it has been an absolute joy.
