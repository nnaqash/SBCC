Meet our helpers!

The helpers of the Scottish Burned Children's Club give their time and skills to the club for different events and allow the club to set up activities smoothly. They are not fully volunteers but are still invaluable to making the club as special as it is. We asked the helpers to write a short description about themselves on how they became involved with the club and why they stick around. Click on the images to read more about the team!
